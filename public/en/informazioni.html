 ACTION

This page will explain you the behavior of the buttons ACTION:

Previous
The system re-selects the previous location and if you are not there neighbors plays a clip that says to reach the place indicated on the map.
Where Am I?
Depending on where you are, the system will play the first clip on that location (Only purpose WHAT ).
Stop
The system stops playback.
More
The system plays the next clip in the list on the same place to visit (Only purpose WHY and purpose HOW ).
Continue
The system resumes playing.
Next
The system chooses the next place to visit and if you are not there neighbors plays a clip that says to reach the place indicated on the map.
